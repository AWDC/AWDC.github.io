Promising Startups to watch for in 2018

Author

Kashish Chauhan
Last Modified- 04/02/2018

Technology start-ups, especially web-based or mobile-based, are flourishing in the modern era. Digital apps in particular continue to rule the scene of modern day entrepreneurship. Unlimited number of start-ups are created every year in the hopes of being the nest Instagram, Facebook or Uber.

So, here’s a list of some of the most promising start-ups to look for in 2018.

eDrone.me

The app is positioned as a CRM, an approach used to manage interaction between the company and its customers - current and potential. The amount of data handled and processed by the system is quite impressive. But this can be due to the fact that they have designed a machine learning algorithm that not only provides insight but also allows one to take actions that increase the life time value of the customer through a number of – simple to use and integrate – tools. It has about a gagillion integrations already, from Magento, Woocommerce to Shopify and Opencart.

They also allow their users to use their app for free which is an added bonus.

TypingDNA

It is now an acceptable truth that “Future is AI” just like the fact that the earth revolves around the sun. The team behind TypingDNA are trying to shape this future in their own way by using machine learning to develop behavioural biometrics solutions. It’s true that there are an unlimited number of applications that are doing this. Imagine not having to remember a password. Ever! TypingDNA are doing this by authenticating a user through his or her typing patterns. They have published certain studies and data that proves that it is wonderful way to secure your system and an easy one at that. One doesn’t require any special hardware or software for this as is the case in most biometrics authentication like retina scan, fingerprint scan, etc.
