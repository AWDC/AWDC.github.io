About Us

About Us

The purpose of Amity Web Designing Club is as follows: -

  • To encourage learning and growth in the field of web development and designing.
  • To create a network of like-minded people who want to pursue web designing.
  • To give back to Amity University, its teachers and students by offering web designing services through several events and workshops.
  • To give back to the web development community by writing and posting about new creations, plugins, extensions, etc.

Why you should join the club?

This one may seem really obvious but joining a club where people have similar interests as you will be beneficial for you. You will get to meet people with similar interests who can gelp you and guide you in your journey. Also you will get to interact with the faculties incharge and entrepreneurs in the field of web development.

What are we good at?

We are quite good at making mobile friendly websites and organizing events. Moreover, The classroom isn't the only learning environment in a school, the skills you learn in stuff like clubs are essential skills that can apply to everyday life. Things such as meeting and speaking to new people, keeping an eye on time, and being organized are things you will use every single day of our lives. Here you will acquire many such skills.

24X7 Avaailable

We are 24X7 available if you need any help regarding the development of the website.

Our Events

Various events are organized by Amity Web Designing Club each year to test and enhance the web designing skills of students at the university. The students with extraordinary skills are awarded for their talent.

Redesign web

Explore your web designing skills and compete them on our platform. Task: Create a website using HTML and CSS on an IDE as attractive as possible. PLEASE CARRY YOUR LAPTOPS WITH NECESSARY SOFTWARES INSTALLED!!

Prometheus

Not only web designing, but the club also organizes events related to other fields in computer science. This time AWDC organized a quiz to test the knowledge of students regarding programming languages, namely java and C++. This event like others was also a huge success and many students participated with great enthusiasm and vigor. Winners were awarded certificates. The event was organized especially for first year students.

Workshop on BOOTSTRAP & PHP

Not only events, the club also organizes many workshops where students can gain knowledge about several topics directly from experts. This time we organized a workshop on Bootstrap and PHP. The event was successful and the students learnt a lot as was our intention.

Workshop on HTML & CSS

One of the first workshops organized by our club was on HTML and CSS. The event was attended by many freshers. The workshop was so well organized and insightful that many students registered on-the-spot after getting the information from our members and fellow students.

Android game Development Workshop

A 3 hour Professional Workshop on: Android Mobile Gaming Application. It ment for students who want to learn android programming and get a good knowledge about android.It gives student opportunity to interact with professionals and industry experts and helps them in the carrer choices and current industry development
